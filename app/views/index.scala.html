About

The idea for this site came from looking at my girlfriend trying to find songs in different tempo to dance to. This lead me to look around for some service which could serve songs based on their bpm's. Since I found nothing around I decided to build one of my own. This is the result.

The template for this site is from Start Bootstrap, and the bpm and danciblity values are provided by the excellent API of the Echo Nest. With playlists via the play button are being graciously provided by Spotify. The fine background photo is by Steve Bernacki (CC BY-NC 2.0).
